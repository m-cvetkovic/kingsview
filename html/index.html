Kingsview Chess Club - Chess is Our Passion

The Kingsview Chess Club is located in Etobicoke, Toronto, ON, Canada. Our members enjoy friendly games of chess every Wednesday evening and we are always looking for new members. Our members vary in ages from 19 and older generally and are at various levels of expertise.

Kingsview Chess Club in been around for over 30 years. It is a friendly club for all levels of chess players. We play several tournaments each season:

Christmas Holiday Blitz
Tournament completed on December 14, 2011. Five rounds, swiss pairing with time control set to 5 minutes. Tournament ended with 3 way tie win between Joe Parisi, Glen Bryan and Ted Robbins. Congratulations to Ted, who won the first place after 3 additional tie breaking games.
Ratings Tournament
Tournament completed on December 7. Additional games will be played to decide first 3 positions. This is swiss-pairing tournament for all members. Time control is 40 moves in 80 minutes + 60 minutes/sudden death (40/4800:3600). Tournaments starts in second week of September each year. Our ratings are unofficial - for fun only, although they are calculated based on official CFC rating rules for established ratings.
Championsip Tournament
Championship Tournament will start on January 11, 2012. We will split into groups of up to 8 members. Matches are played betwee each member in the group.(Double Round-Robin). Time control is same as in Ratings Tournament above (40/4800:3600).
Bill Darch Memorial 1/2hr Tournament
When you are in a hurry, and cannot spend whole Wednesday night, you can play 1/2hr game.

Join us at any Time

We meet every Wednesday evening from 7:45pm-10:45pm at the Franklin Horner Community Centre located on 432 Horner Avenue just east of Browns Line in Etobicoke. Please email us, or call Milan @ 416-837-3968 for more information.
