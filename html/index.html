Kingsview Chess Club - Chess is Our Passion

The Kingsview Chess Club is located in Etobicoke, Toronto, ON, Canada. Our members enjoy friendly games of chess every Wednesday evening and we are always looking for new members. Our members vary in ages and expertise.

Kingsview Chess Club in been around for over 30 years. It is a friendly club for all levels of chess players. We play several tournaments each season:

2014 Championship Tournament 2014
This season Championship Tournament started January 8, 2014. We split in 2 groups of 7 players, Championship Group, and Group A. Matches are played between each member in the group (Double Round-Robin). Time control is 40 moves in 80 minutes.
2013 Ratings Tournament
Tournament started on Wednesday September 12, 2013, and completed after 13 rounds just before Hollidays. Congratulations to Ted with the best score of 6 out of 8 games.
2013 Championship Tournament
Championship Tournament completed in May 2013. We were split in 2 groups of 9 players, Championship Group, and Group A. Matches were played between each member in the group (Round-Robin). Time control is 40 moves in 80 minutes. After 3 way tie, Kyle won the 1st place. Congratulations to our winners Kyle, and Geof.
Bill Darch Memorial 1/2hr Tournament
When you are in a hurry, and cannot spend whole Wednesday night, you can play 1/2hr game.
Christmas Holiday Blitz
Tournament was held on December 12, 2011. Six rounds, swiss pairing with time control set to 5 minutes.
2012 Ratings Tournament
Tournament completed on December 5, 2012. After fierce fight in last couple of rounds, Moe Kamkar emerged as the winner. This is swiss-pairing tournament for all members. Time control is 40 moves in 80 minutes + 60 minutes/sudden death (40/4800:3600). Tournaments starts in second week of September each year. Our ratings are unofficial - for fun only, although they are calculated based on official CFC rating rules for established ratings.

Join us at any Time

We meet every Wednesday evening from 7:45pm-10:45pm at the Franklin Horner Community Centre located on 432 Horner Avenue just east of Browns Line in Etobicoke. Please email us, or call Milan @ 416-837-3968 for more information.
